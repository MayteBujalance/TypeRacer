How to Play TypeRacer
  1. Select your desired difficulty level from the dropdown.
  2. Click Start to begin, or just start typing to start the timer automatically.
  3. Type the displayed text as quickly and accurately as you can.
  4. Correct words will be highlighted in blue, incorrect in red.
  5. Press Enter or click Stop to finish the test.
  6. Click Retry for a new random text at the same level.
